--- DESCRIPTION ---
In today’s changeable environment, teamwork is essential for business success. And high-functioning teams need leaders who can include, facilitate and motivate. 

In this 100% online, self-paced programme, you learn to build strong relationships and gain trust and respect. You also discover coaching skills that will allow you to understand your teams, communicate effectively and inspire them to achieve even more.

## Module 1: Leadership
This programme comprises 7 modules - each diving into a different aspect of people skills. 

Module 1: Leadership
Recognise the role that people skills play in leadership, particularly authentic leadership  
Define authentic leadership 
Identify how your personality is the gateway to authenticity 
Module 2: Your leadership story
Identify how an individual's life story constitutes a frame for understanding how leadership and authenticity go hand-in-hand 
Relate your own personal story and how it might apply to your leadership and authenticity  
Module 3: Authenticity
Identify the different pathways and methods you can use to develop your authenticity 
Recognise how your mindset plays a crucial role in developing authenticity within yourself and in how you interact with others 
Module 4: Becoming a true coach
Understand the importance of coaching in today's business environment
Familiarise yourself with the golden triangle of coaching
Module 5: Setting goals
Apply goal-setting principles in setting goals for yourself and others
Discover the value of delegation within goal setting
Module 6: Feedback
Apply some basic feedback frameworks in delivering feedback to others
Recognise the value of the 'feedback curve' for planning your intervention
Module 7: Support
Recognise the role of emotions in all aspects of leadership and people skills
Develop basic approaches for managing different types of emotions
Programme design 

This online journey brings together learning formats including videos, interviews, exercises, questionnaires and real-life examples. You’ll also have background reading to do and questions to help you reflect on your own experience.
 
You can start Essentials in People Skills at any time – and study wherever and whenever suits you. It takes around 10 to 12 hours to complete the programme and you can split this time over a maximum of three months.

Start for free – no strings attached. This free module gives you a taste of our Essentials programmes. Try it now and explore how Essentials in People Skills helps you lead with confidence, build trust and influence others – all on your own schedule.
