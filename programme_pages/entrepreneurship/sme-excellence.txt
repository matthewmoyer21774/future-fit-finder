--- DESCRIPTION ---
You’ve built a thriving business that’s running at full capacity. You’re both the driving force and the strategist of the company. You’re also keeping a tight rein on things at operational level. It's a lot to deal with. Do you dream of delegating work and making the business less dependent on you? Is it time to invest in management, people and systems so that your SME can keep growing and prospering? This 10-day, programme gives you the support and insights you need to take your business to the next level. With the help of Vlerick experts, peers and business coaches, you’ll develop a business plan that provides a bridge between strategy and daily practice – and that gives you solid foundations to further professionalise and grow your company.

## TRACK 1: MANAGEMENT EDUCATION
This 10-day programme is structured around three pillars. The first is made up of management training with practice-oriented sessions where you apply management theory, models and tools to your own company. The second is an individual coaching programme by business coaches and experts. The third is access to the unique Vlerick learning network of fellow entrepreneurs and the wider Vlerick ecosystem for lifelong learning. You’ll also use the Vlerick Growth Scan to identify the soft spots in your management you can still work on. Then you’ll refine your personal vision for growing your business in a sustainable way.

You’ll also use Vlerick’s 360° growth capability scan to identify the management areas you need to work on. Then you’ll refine your personal vision for growing your business in a sustainable way. 

There are two tracks to follow: 

TRACK 1: MANAGEMENT EDUCATION 
Module 1: Entrepreneurship, growth and strategy 
Understand why strategy is as important as daily operations 
Assess your business model and begin to plan a winning strategy 
Identify your growth phase and the challenges that come with it  
Explore the capabilities you need to achieve sustainable growth 
Visualise the process to translate your strategy into operational KPIs and actions – using tools including Strategy Mapping and Balanced Scorecard 
Module 2: Marketing, sales, and digitisation
Discover how to set up and develop a sales strategy, including performance metrics 
Learn to proactively manage your sales pipeline by evaluating the resources and people you need to meet your targets
Become more customer centric and develop a unique value proposition 
Identify technology that can add value to your business
Module 3: Leadership and people management
Reflect on your leadership skills – and identify areas to improve
Learn to customise your approach to motivate your employees to perform better
Examine how to attract the ‘right’ employees to your organisation
Explore ways to keep talented employees on board – and coach them along the way
Learn to say yes to delegating responsibilities, as well as outsourcing tasks
Module 4: Financial management
Learn to interpret your financials and evaluate your company’s financial health
Understand the questions to ask financial advisors so you can improve financial reporting
TRACK 2: BUSINESS SUPPORT
Carry out a thorough analysis of your current business practices – including your own management performance
Draw up a strategic business plan that maps out your company’s future plans – and translates them into milestones, actions and objectives 
Get expert, tailored advice from a unique panel of experienced business coaches and experts
Take part in three individual coaching sessions – and come away with a concrete business plan for your sme

PRACTICAL INFO

To apply for this programme, you need to complete four steps:

1. Check your eligibility: Check if you meet the admissions criteria for the programme you want to apply for.

2. Submit your application: Fill out the application form and upload it together with your cv or LinkedIn profile.

3. Take part in an interview: When we receive your application, we’ll get in touch to set up an interview. This will take about an hour and will cover:

Your professional experience and current challenges
Your career ambitions
Why you want to take the programme
Which electives are right for you (if the programme includes electives)

4. Confirm your registration: If you pass the interview, we’ll send you a registration form. You’ll have two weeks to complete and submit the form to confirm your programme place.
