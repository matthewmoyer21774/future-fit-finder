--- DESCRIPTION ---
Do you dream of being manager of your own company – where you can put your entrepreneurial spirit and business knowledge and experience to good use? Taking over an existing company could be the ideal way to make your entrepreneurial dream come true. 

But before you dive in, it’s essential to ask the right questions. This programme gives you exceptional value for money. You’ll live and learn practical approaches – so you have the confidence to make the leap and buy.

## Module 1: Why buy a company? And how do you find the right company?
Our aging population means there are increasing numbers of SMEs in need of capable new owners. If you’re thinking of buying in – or buying out – this programme will give you the skills and frameworks to leap with confidence.

Over seven evening modules, you’ll learn practical approaches that you can implement immediately. And you’ll work on your own buyout case and present it to a panel of bankers, private equity players and entrepreneurs. These seven modules represent the chronological steps that you must take to achieve a successful buy-out or buy-in. 

Module 1: Why buy a company? And how do you find the right company?  
Discover the essential questions to ask yourself before you start 
Understand where and how to find suitable targets 
Explore how to view paperwork critically – including confidentiality (non-disclosure) agreements (NDA) and letters of intent (LOI) 
Module 2: Valuation 
Know how much you should pay – and understand different valuation methods 
Learn how to build an LBO model to calculate your ROI 
Explore the challenges and pitfalls when valuing a target company 
Module 3: Business plan, value creation and due diligence 
Learn to play with sources of value creation in the short and long term 
Discover the steps in a good due diligence process – and know how to minimise risks 
Understand how to deal with legal, financial and fiscal issues 
Module 4: Financing 
Learn how to structure the deal  
Understand how to optimise financing costs and minimise risks   
Discover how to convince banks – and what they pay attention to 
Explore the role private equity can play 
Module 5: Transaction 
Discover the legal framework for buyouts – and what you need to pay attention to 
Learn how to best work with advisors 
Explore the safeguards and guarantees to negotiate with the seller 
Module 6: Negotiation techniques
Learn essential negotiation techniques for buying a company
Understand how to reach a good agreement
Discover how to win the seller’s trust
Module 7: Post-transaction
Learn to run an SME
Know how to install new management and develop a new strategy
Discover how to draw up a 100-day plan
Explore typical pitfalls
OUR PARTNERS
OTHER GUEST SPEAKERS AND EXPERTS

Lieven Stas (VD&P), Aelbrecht Van Damme (The Harbour), Kristoph Wauters (Bank van Breda) and more.

Do you dream of being manager of your own company – where you can put your entrepreneurial spirit and business knowledge and experience to good use? Taking over an existing company could be the ideal way to make your entrepreneurial dream come true. 

But before you dive in, it’s essential to ask the right questions. This programme gives you exceptional value for money. You’ll live and learn practical approaches – so you have the confidence to make the leap and buy.

DETAILED PROGRAMME

Our aging population means there are increasing numbers of SMEs in need of capable new owners. If you’re thinking of buying in – or buying out – this programme will give you the skills and frameworks to leap with confidence.

Over seven evening modules, you’ll learn practical approaches that you can implement immediately. And you’ll work on your own buyout case and present it to a panel of bankers, private equity players and entrepreneurs. These seven modules represent the chronological steps that you must take to achieve a successful buy-out or buy-in. 

Module 1: Why buy a company? And how do you find the right company?  
Discover the essential questions to ask yourself before you start 
Understand where and how to find suitable targets 
Explore how to view paperwork critically – including confidentiality (non-disclosure) agreements (NDA) and letters of intent (LOI) 
Module 2: Valuation 
Know how much you should pay – and understand different valuation methods 
Learn how to build an LBO model to calculate your ROI 
Explore the challenges and pitfalls when valuing a target company 
Module 3: Business plan, value creation and due diligence 
Learn to play with sources of value creation in the short and long term 
Discover the steps in a good due diligence process – and know how to minimise risks 
Understand how to deal with legal, financial and fiscal issues 
Module 4: Financing 
Learn how to structure the deal  
Understand how to optimise financing costs and minimise risks   
Discover how to convince banks – and what they pay attention to 
Explore the role private equity can play 
Module 5: Transaction 
Discover the legal framework for buyouts – and what you need to pay attention to 
Learn how to best work with advisors 
Explore the safeguards and guarantees to negotiate with the seller 
Module 6: Negotiation techniques
Learn essential negotiation techniques for buying a company
Understand how to reach a good agreement
Discover how to win the seller’s trust
Module 7: Post-transaction
Learn to run an SME
Know how to install new management and develop a new strategy
Discover how to draw up a 100-day plan
Explore typical pitfalls
