--- DESCRIPTION ---
When you integrate AI tools into your daily work, you can boost your productivity, enhance creativity and make smarter, data-driven decisions.

This programme is designed to help you unlock the potential of AI in your professional life – from improving efficiency, through to idea generation. Over six modules, you’ll explore real-life cases, develop practical skills – and have opportunities to experiment with AI tools that could transform the way you work.

## Module 1: Enhance your productivity
This programme comprises six modules, each designed to give you tools and strategies to leverage AI in your daily work. Through a blend of self-paced learning and live online sessions, you won’t just learn about AI – you’ll begin to actively apply it.

Module 1: Enhance your productivity
Understand the core concepts and capabilities of AI
Explore how GenAI models generate content
Dive into current AI trends 
Module 2: Master data skills
Use AI to generate, structure, summarise and analyse complex data sets
Create compelling data visualisations to communicate insights
Experiment with cutting-edge AI tools for advanced data analysis
Module 3: Become a creative champion
Use AI tools to generate ideas
Master techniques to refine and evaluate AI-generated concepts
Learn how to integrate AI into brainstorming and other creative processes
Module 4: Make better decisions
Explore how AI can help to assess business options and scenarios
Learn to use AI to make more informed, data-driven decisions
Discuss the ethical implications of using AI in decision making
Module 5: Redefine customer experience
Discover how AI can give customers a faster and more personalised service
Explore how AI can enhance different forms of customer engagement
Module 6: Agentic AI & the future
Analyse the key principles, applications, and limitations of Agentic AI.
Evaluate, select and implement Agentic AI to transform specific workflows.
Apply futures studies techniques to forecast emerging trends and societal impacts of AI.
