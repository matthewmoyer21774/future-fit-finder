--- DESCRIPTION ---
Society is evolving. And the business case for sustainability is growing ever stronger. So organisations are rethinking strategy, and aligning purpose with performance.

This 100% online, self-paced programme sets out how sustainability and profitability can go hand in hand. Take part to discover concepts, frameworks and stakeholder engagement strategies, build a roadmap and navigate ESG reporting. You’ll gain the confidence to play a key role in your organisation’s sustainability transformation.

## Module 1: Balancing purpose and profit
This programme comprises four modules, each focusing on a different aspect of sustainability management.

Module 1: Balancing purpose and profit
Understand how profit and sustainability can reinforce each other
Discover how a business founded on purpose can create both profit and social value
Module 2: Framing sustainability and stakeholder engagement
Get to know the “sustainability dictionary” – understand terms including ESG, sustainable value creation, scope 1-2-3 emissions, circular economy, philanthropy and more
Identify which stage of sustainability your company is in
Learn how double materiality and stakeholder engagement can help set sustainability-focused priorities
Module 3: Designing your sustainability strategy
Define the role of a sustainability strategy – and understand its importance 
Explore the differences between sustainability strategies created at tactical, business unit and corporate level
Develop the cornerstones of your sustainability strategy
Module 4: Introducing sustainability reporting
Understand why sustainability reporting matters
Explore the value of disclosing non-financial, ESG-related information
Get to know the main sustainability reporting frameworks

Programme design

Start Essentials in Sustainability Management at any time – and study wherever and whenever suits you. It takes around 8-10 hours to complete the programme, and you have a maximum of three months to go through all the modules. 

It’s an online journey that’s packed with videos, interviews, exercises, questionnaires and real-life examples. You’ll also have background reading to do – and questions to help you reflect on your own experiences.
