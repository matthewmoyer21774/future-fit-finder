--- DESCRIPTION ---
The business world is changing rapidly. To stay ahead, organisations need to be ready to disrupt, transform – and roll out new business ideas rapidly. They need to make innovation part of business as usual.

This three-day programme introduces you to the tools you need to design, implement and manage an innovation strategy that leads to business growth. You live and learn what it means to be an inspiring innovation leader – so you can leap into embedding an innovative culture in your organisation.

DETAILED PROGRAMME

## MODULE 1: INNOVATION AMBITION – DEVELOPING  YOUR VISION
We start from the foundation of theoretical concepts and insights,  and then we inspire you to apply these concretely to your own business situation to develop an innovation strategy – with a focus  on practicality – for your organisation. The programme ‘Leading Innovation’ is comprised of  3 modules:

MODULE 1: INNOVATION AMBITION – DEVELOPING  YOUR VISION 
Identify your corporate innovation ambition 
Explore the impact of living in times of exponential change 
Identify ways to make your company, product or service more  relevant 
Dive into the dynamics that lead to disruptive, industry-shaking innovations 
Lead for innovation and create the right team culture for innovations
Create a (team) culture that is conducive for idea generation,  how to promote ideas and manage the corporate stakeholders 
Drive the implementation of innovative ideas 
MODULE 2:  INNOVATION CONCEPT – ORGANISING  YOUR INNOVATION PROCESSES 
Discover how to manage your core business for sustained  innovation 
Learn to manage an innovation project portfolio 
Dive into corporate venturing and business model innovation 
How digital technologies change the markets’ structure 
Tools beyond Osterwalder canvas: Ecosystem perspective on  business models • Pros and cons of the most important digital business model patterns 
MODULE 3: PLANNING FOR INNOVATION 
Take part in an innovation strategy simulation: make tough product portfolio decisions and learn to allocate your innovation budget 
Build and maintain a dynamic ‘Horizons of Growth’ innovation  project portfolio  
Specifying what to do by when in each horizon 
Specifying which projects to promote or stop in each horizon
