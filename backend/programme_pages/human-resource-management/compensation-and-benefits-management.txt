--- DESCRIPTION ---
Want to motivate your teams to focus on your organisation’s strategic objectives? But don’t know the best approach to remuneration? If you’re an HR professional who wants to develop a comprehensive understanding of strategic rewarding, this programme is for you. 

The way your company approaches compensation and benefits can make a real difference to performance. So discover how to drive motivation and engagement – and get the dynamics of reward and compensation working for you.

## Module 1: Strategic rewards unveiled
Gain a deep understanding of the thinking and techniques you need to align reward to your company’s strategic goals. In this highly-engaging programme, you’ll interact with your peers and experts, take part in discussions and share insights. 

Over 5 thought-provoking days, you’ll learn approaches that you can implement immediately to create real impact in your organisation. You’ll enhance your company – and your career.
