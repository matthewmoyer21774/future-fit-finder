--- DESCRIPTION ---
Moving into a management role is an exciting time. But without leadership development training, it can be daunting too. That’s why we designed the Leadership Transition Programme.

It blends two high-impact programmes – Self-leadership and The People Manager  – with individual, expert coaching. The result is a powerful 6.5-day learning experience that features high-quality, transformational content at every step. You’ll gain the skills and confidence to step into your management role.

Interested in just one programme? You can also follow Self-leadership and The People Manager as individual programmes.

## Track I: Self-leadership - Managing yourself
Over 6.5 days, you’ll follow two leadership programmes – Self-leadership (two days) and The People Manager (four days). You’ll also benefit from a one-to-one coaching session – tailored to your personal goals.

You’ll discover ways to manage yourself effectively. You’ll become a better manager of others. And through expert coaching, you’ll identify and address your own strengths and weaknesses and create a plan to move forward.

Learn how to customise performance management
Discover how to maximise impact
Track III: Individual coaching and action plan

Together you’ll work on setting goals and identifying opportunities to bring your leadership management skills and leadership training into your own organisation.
