--- DESCRIPTION ---
Change is a given in today’s business world. Yet many change management plans do not succeed.

This 3,5-day learning journey gives you the tools and approaches to create positive energy for change. You’ll live and learn new approaches – and take the leap to becoming a sought-after catalyst for change, all with the support of our expert faculty.

It’s the perfect programme for professionals who want to understand the change management process and change control.

DETAILED PROGRAMME

## Module 1: Introduction to change (online)
Over 3,5 intensive days, you’ll learn approaches, skills and techniques that you can apply directly in your workplace. The learning format blends on campus, online, face-to-face and on-the-job activities. And throughout it all, you’ll have expert guidance from our faculty, who are experts in leading change. 

The programme gives you a unique opportunity to learn new ways of approaching change projects – so that you can have greater impact in your organisation, and enhance your career. 

This programme is offered in a blended format, covering both on-campus and online sessions.

Module 1: Introduction to change (online) 
Learn to move from sequential to inclusive thinking about change management 
Discover the Vlerick Six Batteries of Change model, including a quick scan of your own organisation 
Explore your own change project – and propose your Change Master Plan   
Module 2: Leading change (on campus) 
Discover the rational and emotional batteries of the Six Batteries of Change model 
Explore the actions you need to take to set up a change management project in your organisation 
Discover a toolbox, complete with templates, to use in your organisation 
Module 3: Managing change in your organisation (online) 
Dive deep into your Change Master Plan 
Learn from feedback and input from experienced faculty and peers 
Be supported and prepared as you get ready to lead change in your organisation
