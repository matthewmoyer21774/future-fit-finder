--- DESCRIPTION ---
As a sales professional, you’re driving the growth of your business. To get maximum growth for your company, you need the right mix of skills and experience.

This five-day programme turns talented sales professionals into exceptional sales leaders. It helps you develop your leadership skills in a sustainable and responsible way – giving you opportunities to accelerate your career, get better results – and lead sales teams to success.

## Module 1: Sales Management – Make your sales team more effective
The Sales Leadership Programme is designed to help you tackle both your own and your organisation’s challenges. You’ll live and learn how to manage, coach and motivate your sales force – so you can leap into a selling strategy and realise top line growth. And you’ll explore your strengths and weaknesses as a sales leader. 

Over two modules, you’ll master the essential skills you need to lead sales teams to even greater success. 

Module 1: Sales Management – Make your sales team more effective  
Develop a Sales Management Framework 
Dive into go-to-market strategy 
Explore key sales motivators 
Discover approaches to enhance performance management 
Module 2: Self-leadership – Develop your own unique management style  
Analyse your management approach using the Vlerick Cognitive Style Indicator 
Learn to deal with different personalities in a professional context 
Explore team dynamics and how they impact individual behaviour 
Understand yourself – and how self-management helps you lead others
